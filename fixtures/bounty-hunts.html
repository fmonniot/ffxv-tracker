    Bounty Hunts

    Edit Page    Last Edit: January 22, 2017 - 25 days 12 hours ago

    Similar to Side Quests, Final Fantasy XV includes Bounty Hunts that are specialized missions where Noctis and his pals must track down and defeat specified creatures roaming the wilds of Eos. Unlike Side Quests, these Hunts are only found from "tipsters" who run various diners, restaurants, and eateries across the land. Only one Hunt can be active at a time - but the bounty can be turned in to any tipster.

    Bounties are also classified by rank, as Noctis will start at rank 1, clearing bounties will earn stars that advance his rank and allow him to undertake more dangerous hunts - and unlock new ones as well. For more information on the various hunts, see the lists below classified by area and recommended level.
